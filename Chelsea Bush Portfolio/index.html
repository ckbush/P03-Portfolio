About Me

I’m Chelsea, a visual designer based in Vancouver, BC. Currently, I’m a third year design student in the School of Interactive Arts and Technology at Simon Fraser University. For me, design acts as my creative outlet, as well as an opportunity to share meaningful and engaging experiences with others. Through my work, I strive to bring a unique perspective to every new design challenge.

Despite spending most of my days at the computer, I am also very passionate about getting back to nature. When I’m not designing, I can typically be found hiking or snowboarding somewhere within the beautiful landscape of BC.
